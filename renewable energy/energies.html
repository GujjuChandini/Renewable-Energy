TYPES OF RENEWABLE ENERGY

Solar Energy

Solar photovoltaic(PV) devices, or solar cells, convert sunlight directly into electricity.Solar energy systems do not produce air pollutants or carbondioxide. Solar energy systems on building have minimal effects on the environment.

Geothermal Energgy

Geothermal energy is heat within the earth.The word geothermal comes from the Greek word geo(earth) and therme(heat).Geothermal energy is a renewable energy source because heat is continously produced inside the earth.

Biomass

Biomass is renewable organic material that comes from plants and animals.Biomass continues to be an important fuel in many countries, especially for cooking and heating in developing countries.

Wind Energy

Wind is caused by uneven heating of earth's surface by the sun.Because the earth's surface is made up of different types of land and water, the earth absorbs the sun's heat at different rates.

Hydro Power

Hydropower was one of the first sources of energy used for electricity generation. Solar energy heats water on surface of rivers,lakes,and oceans,which causes the water to evaporate
